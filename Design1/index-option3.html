WELCOME TO FRASER UNIQUIP

Fraser Uniquip is a privately owned Company with our main offices located in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia and Sydney, Australia.With a history reaching back to 1957 the Fraser Uniquip Group has over 50 years of experience in Process Equipment design and supply to the Oil, Gas, Petrochemical and Mineral Processing Industries

With a commitment to Safety and Quality and a focus on Customer satisfaction, Fraser Uniquip is able to provide innovative designs based on robust and proven technology. using the latest industry accepted software complimented by our own In House software which has been developed over many years of successful plant operation.
